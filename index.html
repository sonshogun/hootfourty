The Library

I've been on a journey to catalog all my games, so if you are excited to try something you used to play you can see if I have it below.

Feel free to bring your own old stuff if you like as well and I'm sure we can hook it up!

The MiSTer

The MiSTer project is a cool open sourced attempt to use FPGA hardware to emulate old consoles and computers.

My rig for it can play games and is loaded with ROMs for the NES, SNES, Genesis, Playstation, Sega Master System, and much more!

MegaHAL

Thanks to interested parties I have in my possession the brain of an AI which hasn't been turned on since the early 00's - let's see what it has to say!
